REVIEW

Brandon Talbot January 31st, 2021


The movie assumes that audiances have seen the previous movies that deal with each characters back story. If someone is watching this movie and it's the first marvel movie they have ever seen; they may be a little lost in the beginning since they will be trying to figure out who all the characters are in the movie.

The movie has great action sences with giant battles and impossible odds which adds a feel of excitment and desperation to the movie. There is the challenges of creating a new team with people who normally work by them selves and figuring out how to put there differences asside for the greater good of the planet.
